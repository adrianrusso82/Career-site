About

Fonteva develops innovative solutions for non-profits, associations, colleges and universities using the Salesforce platform. We are on the forward edge of association management software and push the limits of cloud-based AMS solutions.

Why does Fonteva host this Meetup group? Because we enjoy interacting with the Salesforce development community. Our events are great opportunities to learn new techniques, methods, frameworks or just "talk tech" with industry leaders. Come join us and see why Fonteva is the industry leader in cloud-based association management software.
